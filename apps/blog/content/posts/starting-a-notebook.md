Every blog starts with a post nobody needs. This is that one.

I built this site to have somewhere to put things down. It's a notebook that happens to be public: when I think I've understood something, writing it out is how I find out whether I really have. Often I haven't, and the writing is where that becomes obvious.

## What goes here

Architecture decisions, and the reasons behind them. Notes on tooling, which is where I spend more of my week than I'd admit. Post-mortems, including the ones where the mistake was mine.

Sometimes the site itself. It's an Nx monorepo with a React Router app, its own design tokens and a small component library, and it has already taught me a few things I didn't expect to learn from a blog.

## What doesn't

Hot takes. I have them, but they go stale faster than I can publish them.

That's the whole plan. The rest is still being written.
