Typography

Two families: Space Grotesk for every piece of text, and JetBrains Mono for labels, metadata, dates and tags. Sizes are consumed through the --typography-* semantic roles, never through the raw --text-* scale.

Text — Space Grotesk

The body role carries the long-form rhythm: a generous 1.75 leading that keeps an article readable at the prose width of 42.5rem. This is the default size of the document — everything else is a deviation from it.

--typography-body-size
1rem / --text-base
leading --typography-body-leading (1.75)
width --layout-prose-width (42.5rem)

Small text — secondary copy, captions, skill chips. Muted by default.

--typography-small-size
0.875rem / --text-sm
color --color-muted-foreground
